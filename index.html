Connecting Young Minds with Mathematical Joy

Supplements math education for students in the New Rochelle community through interactive exploration, problem-solving puzzles, and friendly competition.

Community Notice: NewRo Solves proudly supports and supplements local math education for families within the City School District of New Rochelle, operating as an independent community organization without direct district affiliation.

Who We Are

NewRo Solves introduces students to joyful, advanced mathematical concepts outside the typical school curriculum. We build critical thinking, mathematical intuition, and collaborative problem-solving frameworks. Our programs are brought to life with the incredible support of local high school student volunteers who help mentor younger mathematicians.

Elementary Program (K-5)

Hosted monthly at the New Rochelle Public Library, this initiative focuses on open exploration.


  • Mathematical logic games & strategic puzzles
  • Hands-on pattern recognition activities
  • Low-floor, high-ceiling problem tasks

Middle School Program (6-8)

Tailored specifically for local middle school students looking for deeper geometric and algebraic challenges.


  • Math Olympiad preparation
  • Advanced team problem-solving strategies
  • Competitive game theory simulations

Community & STEM Outreach

We actively partner with regional schools to spread the love of math throughout Westchester County.


  • Interactive booths at local school STEM nights
  • Engaging family-focused math puzzles
  • Community math circles and resource sharing
